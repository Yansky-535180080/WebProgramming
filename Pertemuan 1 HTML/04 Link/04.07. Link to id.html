Film-Making terms

Arc-shot Interlude Prologue

Arc-shot

A shot in which the subject i photographed by an encircling or moving camera.

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequences, not specificaly tied to the plot, that appears within a film.

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or a brief scene preceeding the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue.
